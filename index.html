AWESOME WEB DESIGNER & DEVELOPER!

Hi, my name is Marjon. I am currently enrolled in the Ironhack web development bootcamp in Barcelona, coding awesome things. For the past 3 years I have worked as a web designer for an online marketing company in Amsterdam. With a desire to further develop myself professionally, I decided to part ways.   Learning how to code has been a crazy and mostly a fun ride, especially now that I get to put everything together and build great apps!

As I am about to finish the programming bootcamp, I am currently looking for my next adventure. I am looking to work with a company where I can be a part of creating a product or service that brings people together and makes their day-to-day lives easier with the use of well designed applications.

I’m willing to relocate worldwide, as long as there's coffee to support me while designing my next great UI project!

Why you should hire me

I love great designand I'm picky about it too.. I think a good experience can easily be disrupted by pixels that are off, colors that don't match or fonts that just don't work. One of the aspects of good design to me is consistency, even down to the littlest detail.

Creating a terrific user experience must be one of my favorite parts of being a designer. I love the challenge of creating an experience that is effortless and intuitive to the user. With my eye for detail, ability to empathize with the user and my design and development skill set I can be a valuable addition to your team.

With some years of design experienceand my newly gained programming knowledge I am ready for the next challenge. Ideally this would be in a UX team where I can create beautiful user interfaces while diving deeper in to some programming languages.
